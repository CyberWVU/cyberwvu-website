About CyberWVU

Cyber WVU is a student run organization that started in September of 2011 by James Mantheiy and Dr. Roy Nutter with the goal to spread computer forensic and cyber security skills to the student body of West Virginia University in Morgantown. CyberWVU was and still is the only student run organization of it's kind in the state of West Virginia.

Our goal is simple: broaden the awareness of cyber security and its importance. We can generate intrest and excitement through organizing and competing in various cyber defense competitions. We can better equip general users in their day-to-day interaction with technology by holding workshops on practicing cyber safety. Finally, we aim to build a legacy of cyber defense that attracts students to our program and promotes West Virginia University as a university on the cutting edge of cyber security

CyberWVU also competes in a variety of competitions such as the National Cyber League and the National Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition. These competitions help develop both offensive and defensive skills. We believe that in order to better prepare against attacks, you must have knowledge of how they will occur. Only then can you properly prepare and defend against incoming attacks.
