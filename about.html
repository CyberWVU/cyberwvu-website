About CyberWVU

CyberWVU - A cyber security and computer forensics group.

Who are we?

Cyber WVU is a student run organization that started in September of 2011 by James Mantheiy and Dr. Roy Nutter with the goal to spread computer forensic and cyber security skills to the student body of West Virginia University in Morgantown. CyberWVU was and still is the only student run organization of it's kind in the state of West Virginia. CyberWVU members can be from any major and any background.

What do we do?

CyberWVU is a student organization at West Virginia University. Our primary objective is to broaden the awareness of cyber security and its importance. We generate an awareness by competing annually in two different competitions. We want to inspire and promote students to protect themselves and their data with our message.

How can You get involved?

Getting involved is easy! Check out the what's new page to see if we have an event coming up, attend one of our weekly meetings, or contact one of our officers. Everyone is welcome. We are always happy to see new faces!
